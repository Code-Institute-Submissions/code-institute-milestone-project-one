Mix Process

File Transfer

When you have a song that's ready for me to work on, get in touch with me to discuss rates. Different projects require different amounts of time so I'll cater that to your needs. Once that's settled, I will send over a document that explains how to prepare the stems for me to work with. When the stems and rough mix has been sent, I can get started!

Mix Process

Depending on the size of the project, I aim to get a mix back over within two/ three days. I offer three rounds of revisions per mix as standard, so once you send a detailed list of notes over I can begin to make tweaks.

Review

Once the mix is complete and you're happy, I will send over:
1. A full bounce of the song in whatever formats you need.
2. A vocal + 1db mix.
3. An instrumental mix.
4. A TV mix.
5. An acapella track.
I also print my own group stems of each mix (Drums, Bass, Guitar etc.) that I can send over if you'd like them.

Pricing

I aim to cater my price for Mixing/ Recording to match your requirements and budget. Each project is different so I've found this to be the fairest way to make sure you get the most for your money. For Mastering, I charge £30 for a single but, like Mixing, I am flexible with EP/ Album projects. Once a fee has been agreed, I will send over a working contract and an invoice. For any details and a quote, please get in touch.
