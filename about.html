Bio

I am Daryl Johnson. I'm a freelance engineer based in London. So far in my short career, I have worked with a vast number of artists, going above and beyond to provide the best service possible. Working at Metropolis Studios for six and a half years, I have been entrusted to work with artists such as Pharrell Williams, The Who and Drake amongst many others on numerous occasions. Across this time, I worked with some of the top engineers and producers in the industry, learning many techniques first hand from the best in the business, that I can apply to your next project.

Experience

I have worked at the highest end of the music industry for seven years. From recording orchestras to midnight rap sessions; long haul album projects to live to vinyl recordings - I really have seen it all. Working with the biggest names across a wide array of genres.

A Helping Hand

Unsure about a part? Think a certain chord sequence could use some help? I have been writing and producing music solo and in bands for many years, and will be more than willing to help your music reach its full potential. Another set of ears is priceless!

Flexible

The nature of the industry is ever-changing, and I do everything I can to accomodate whatever a client may need. With competitive pricing and an honest appraisal of your projects needs, I strive to make sure you get exactly where you want to be!
