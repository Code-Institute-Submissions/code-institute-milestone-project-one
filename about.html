Bio

Daryl Johnson is a freelance engineer based in London. So far in his short career, he has worked with a vast number of artists, going above and beyond to provide the best service possible. Working at Metropolis Studios for six and a half years, Daryl has been entrusted to work with artists such as Pharrell Williams, The Who and Drake amongst many others on numerous occasions. Across this time, Daryl worked with some of the top engineers and producers in the industry, learning many techniques first hand from the best in the business.
